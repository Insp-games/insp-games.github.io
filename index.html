Our games

H377

An exciting turn-based strategy in a world of eternal apocalypse. Mix technology and dark magic to create super soldiers resistant to harsh battle conditions. This world has long been a battlefield in which only the strongest creatures can survive, and who they will be is up to you.

Gather an army of incredible creatures and set out to conquer uncharted territories. In the end, only those who can adapt will remain.

Vampires and Dragons - Collectible card game

Collectible card game about the conflict between fantastic dragons and the undead fighting for territory and resources. Special crystals give indescribable strength and power. The outcome of the battle in this fairy-tale world depends on you.

Take part in the battle and gather your own army to participate in epic battles for the right to be the first ruler in this universe.

Dig Muck: Craft Adventure

Welcome to Muck Island, an enigmatic and vividly colorful land filled with secrets waiting to be discovered. As a lone survivor, your primary objective is to endure as long as possible in this surreal, adventure-filled world. Unearth resources, craft vital tools and weapons, and protect yourself against relentless enemies that emerge under the cover of night.

Dive deep into the life of a survivor on Muck Island.
